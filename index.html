Safeguard Mechanism 2024-25 — what the numbers say versus what they mean

The headline fell. Covered emissions dropped 2.91 Mt, baselines tightened 9.21 Mt, and facilities beat their targets on paper. But a handful of unplanned shutdowns did most of the work, offset surrenders hit a record, and the population of facilities churned more than any single-year comparison suggests. This dashboard shows every figure from the CER table, with every rename, new entrant and dropout accounted for.
